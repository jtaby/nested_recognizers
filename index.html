Overview:

This demo showcases how multi-touch nested gesture recognizers work. The blue div is a child view of the red div. Both red view and blue view have pan and pinch gesture recognizers. Both are 2-finger gestures.

Instructions:

  • Use two fings to pinch/pan the blue nested box
  • Use two fings to pinch/pan the red container box

Things to try:

  • Observe behavior when you put one finger in one view, and a second finger afterwards on another view. This demo highlights the ability to nest gesture recognizers and the bubbling behavior of gesture recognizers.
