Overview:

This demo showcases how multi-touch nested gesture recognizers work. The blue div is a child view of the red div. The red div has a pinch gesture recognizer, while the blue div has a pan gesture recognizer. Both are 2-finger gestures.

Instructions:

  • Use two fings to pan the blue nested box
  • Use two fings to pinch the red container box

Things to try:

  • Observe behavior when you put one finger in one div, and a second finger afterwards on another div.
